<Her voice is rife with mystery and misdirection>

"You are the Doomed One, and I am Fraud. Trust me if you
wish. Or maybe, your wish is to trust me. These are my
subjects, this is my land, and you trespass upon it."

"No one has passed this way in ages, and for that, I will
spare you, for now."

"I can sense what it is you want from me, but as you
already know I cannot give it without payment. I have
three riddles, do you have three answers? More importantly,
are they correct?"

"Do you wish to dance this game of knowledge and chance?"

"I wish you luck, traveler."
