Sharp. Sharp eyes caught yours. As you stare, you realize
that they aren't staring back. They're not staring at all
in fact. Taking in your surroundings, you see that those aren't 
eyes but the wings of a butterfly. These wins belong to Fraud.
His body is cover ever so slightly by a thin black mist. With
a slip and a flap his wings shakes off a layer of blue dust.
He looks at you with amusement, curious about your fate.
